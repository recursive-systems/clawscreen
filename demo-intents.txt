A2UI Prompt-to-Screen v1 demo prompts

1) Give me a calm morning command center with only top priorities.
   Expected dynamic behavior: sparse layout, low-noise wording, high-priority blocks only.

2) Show me everything I need before leaving in 20 minutes.
   Expected dynamic behavior: countdown/time-sensitive blocks become prominent, actionable checklist appears.

3) I’m stressed. Simplify my day into 3 actions.
   Expected dynamic behavior: compressed output, fewer blocks, explicit 3-step structure.

4) Family evening mode: dinner, pickups, tomorrow prep.
   Expected dynamic behavior: evening-oriented grouping with sequence/order emphasis.

5) Show a concise executive dashboard for today.
   Expected dynamic behavior: KPI/summary-oriented blocks, concise metrics and status rollups.
